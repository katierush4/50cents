How We Accomplished This

After a long brainstorming session and the kanban exercise, our team was able to create a specific plan, to create the website that we imagined. We delegated various tasks to different group members-- Katie and Caylynne working on the home page and css, Emma working on the trivia game, and Melody working on a search bar and profile page. Each person went to work and was able to accomplish a ton in the two weeks we had to work on our project.
