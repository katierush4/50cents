Girls Who Code is a nonprofit organization which aims to support and increase the number of women in computer science. AT&T, as well as other big companies help to sponsor this progam and provide many oppurtunities for women. Donate to this program, to help women in tech!






The Malala fund works towards a world where every girl can learn and lead without fear. Whether it be through advocating for girl's education, investing inlocal education activits, or amplifying girl's stories in their own words. Founded by Malala Yousafazi, a Pakistani student and activist from the age of 11, she has worked to create a fund to battle inequality and provide oppurtunities for women.
